Click and Count

A Helpful Took For Awkward Situations

Have you ever found yourself in a social situation where you feel out of place and awkward? Don't know what to do with your hands or where to look? Here is where Click and Count comes to the rescue! Occupy yourself on the phone with this tool when you find yourself unbearably awkward. Less frustrating than the Google Dinosaur Jump game, and more exciting than the calculator app. You can thank me later.
